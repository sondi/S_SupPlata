You guide , Jon Silver

Jon Silver, known to the locals as Plata (aka Silver), is a seasoned professional who knows the water better than anyone else around. Jon grew up swimming and surfing the beaches of Southern California and has acquired a varied skill set. He has resided in Puerto Escondido for the past 25 years and is the loving father to 2 children. Jon speaks both English and Spanish and has a passion to teach, share and coach. Whether you are a beginner or a pro, Jon can help you develop and improve your game and be comfortable in and on the water.

Jon has in depth knowledge of the waters around Puerto Escondido and his outgoing personality along with his relaxed attitude will lend itself to a memorable paddle boarding experience.

Your photographers and yoga instructors

Luz Mendoza & Romina Ruiz are two designers, photograohers and yoga enthusiast. They’ll provide you entretainment and beautiful memorabilia of the tour and your experience.

Our Home Base

SUP Plata tours originate out of La Alejandria – a tropical paradise that is accessed on coastal highway #200 at KM 125 which is 15 minutes west of Puerto Escondido (8 minutes west of the Puerto Escondido airport). This lush location has food service, alcoholic beverages, WIFI and is the storage site for our boards as well as the launching location. After a brief safety and information session, Jon will take you on your paddleboard tour. After your tour, you can sit back and relax, have some food and a cold beer and enjoy the quiet ambience that La Alejandria has to offer.

Our Our Equipment

We have 5 beautiful new paddleboards for tour use. Three of the boards are Angulo Design paddleboards - specifically the “Big Dawg” model. These boards are high quality custom boards that are 12 feet long and 34.5 inches wide offering exceptional stability regardless of your abilities. There is also an 11'11" board and a 10'10" board that can accommodate one to three fins and are optimal for a more experienced paddler and are great for the ocean as well as the laguna.

We have also gone with high end paddles by Kialoa. These paddles are lightweight and fully adjustable to fit any size of paddler.

Tour Information

Our main paddleboard tours consist of two hours on the Laguna Manieltepec during which time you will experience the tranquility and natural beauty of this unique coastal mangrove lagoon located within 15 minutes of Vivo Resorts. The lagoon is 15 km long and has over 150 species of birds. This large, beautiful lake is situated between the foothills of the Sierra Madre del Sur and the Pacific Coast. Surrounded by thick leafy jungle, tall swaying palm trees and mangroves that reach 15 meters of height, you will enjoy a truly unique experience.

Tours can be booked for the AM or PM. A minimum booking of 2 people and it is $650 (pesos) per person for a two hour tour. Transportation to the laguna is available separately for an additional charge.
